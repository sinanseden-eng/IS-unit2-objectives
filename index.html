Big Question: What is the Value of MONEY?

This unit explores the world of money, consumerism, and decision-making. Students will analyze why prices change, how money shapes our plans, and how we can use money to help others.

1. Vocabulary Focus

2. Target Language: Future Tenses

The Future in the Past:

"He **was going to** get a degree..." (Past plans)

"An exchange program **would be** a huge adventure." (Past predictions)

"I **was leaving** for the USA in just three days!" (Past arrangements)

Future Tenses with *will*:

"I know I'**ll have** a job that I love." (Future Simple)

"I'**ll be making** a difference in the lives of patients." (Future Continuous)

"Next year, I'**ll have gotten** my degree." (Future Perfect)

"My friends **will have been working** for years." (Future Perfect Continuous)
